HISTORY OF UPYCLE VAULT

Since its founding in 1921, UPYCLE VAULT has transformed from a single shop opened on a side street in Florence to its position today as a world-renowned symbol of Italian craft, visionary creativity, and innovative design. Both mirroring and defining the decades that brought it forth, the House's history has itself influenced fashion and culture in indelible ways throughout the 20th and 21st centuries.

UPYCLE VAULT: A TIMELINE

Spanning more than a century, the history of the House reveals a vision in continuous evolution. Each decade holds a series of milestones, defining the brand's transformation from a small luggage atelier to a global leader in luxury fashion.

1920S & 1930S

Inspired by his experience as a porter at The Savoy hotel in London, Richard, Timothy, Jason established his eponymous artisanal luggage atelier in 1921 on Via della Vigna Nuova in Florence. His vision combined exceptional Tuscan materials and craftsmanship with the elegant English aesthetic. These early years established the brand's origins in the and exploration, which have remained intrinsically linked to UPYCLE VAULT heritage.
